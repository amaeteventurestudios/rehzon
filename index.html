01
Package-Integrated Sensing
Simultaneously detects temperature excursion, tamper, spoilage, and fill-state from a single sensor element embedded in packaging. No batteries. No wires. External to container. Mass-producible.
02
Battery-Free Thermal Verification
Verifies thermal exposure wirelessly via dielectric change measurement. Damage tolerant. Flexible form factor. Tunable temperature range. No electrical connections required.
03
Material Anomaly Detection
Detects surface and subsurface anomalies — hidden damage, moisture intrusion, and material-state changes invisible to surface inspection or standard logging.
04
Passive Checkpoint Identity
Long-range and wide-coverage passive identity at custody transfer points — without GPS and without tag power. Both range and coverage simultaneously, which conventional RFID cannot deliver.
05
Anomaly-Aware AI
Learns nominal behavior from archived sensor data. Flags real-time deviation without requiring examples of failure. Active alerting before compromised materiel reaches the next checkpoint.
06
Governed Provenance
Every scan, every pass/fail, every anomaly flag flows into a governed record with full lineage and cross-domain data relationships — decision-grade data for enterprise planning.
